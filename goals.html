My Goals

My goals help me stay focused and motivated, and they give me a clear path for the future. My main goal right now is to finish my senior year of high school strong, both academically and mentally, so I will be fully prepared for my first year of college. I want to make sure I am organized, manage my time well, and stay disciplined so I can handle the challenges ahead. Once I start college, my goal is to join organizations that will support my future career and help me grow as a student and professional. I have been researching clubs at my college and found that they have an Accounting Association, which I would like to join because it will give me opportunities to learn from others, gain hands-on experience, and make connections that could help my career. After graduating from college in 2030, I plan to earn my CPA license within a year, so by 2031 or 2032, I hope to become a Certified Public Accountant. Achieving this goal will improve my job opportunities, increase my earning potential, and allow me to work in a field I am passionate about. Another important goal for 2026 is to get my own car by December, which will give me independence, help me manage my time better, and allow me to take on more responsibilities. I also want to focus on personal growth by learning new skills, building strong relationships, and staying positive even when challenges come my way. These goals keep me motivated every day and remind me that with hard work and dedication, I can achieve the future I am working toward.
