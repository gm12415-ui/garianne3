My Skills

I have developed a variety of skills that help me succeed both in school and in my personal life. One skill I am especially proud of is the ability to do my own braids. This not only allows me to express my creativity but also saves me a lot of money, since hairstyles from a stylist can cost up to $500 depending on the pricing. By doing my own hair, I can style it whenever I want, spending only on supplies, which usually cost less than $100. I am proud of this skill because not everyone can do their own hair, so it feels like a personal accomplishment. I also do my own lashes, which saves me another $100 per month, and combined with doing my own hair, I save a significant amount of money each month. In addition to these personal skills, I also have professional skills that help me succeed in group settings. I am a strong team player, I work well with others by treating them with respect, and I always take responsibility for my part in any group project. These skills help me manage my time, money, and relationships effectively while also showing independence and responsibility.
